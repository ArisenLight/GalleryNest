Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 17, 2025

GalleryNest ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information and uploaded content.

Information We Collect

  • Account Info: Name, email address, and password (securely stored via Firebase Authentication).
  • Uploaded Content: Photos uploaded by photographers are stored securely on Firebase Storage.
  • Usage Data: We may collect usage data (e.g. number of uploads, gallery views) to improve our services.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and maintain your client gallery dashboard
  • To send notifications related to account activity
  • To ensure your galleries remain private and secure
  • To track usage based on your subscription

Storage & Security

We use Firebase Hosting, Authentication, and Storage to securely manage your data. Images are stored in private buckets and only accessible to authenticated users or via share links explicitly created by you.

Sharing Your Data

We do not sell or share your personal data or images with third parties. You have full control over whether galleries are shared publicly or remain private.

Your Rights

  • You can request deletion of your account and data at any time
  • You may download your content via share links or authenticated access

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. We will notify users of any significant changes via email or dashboard notices.
